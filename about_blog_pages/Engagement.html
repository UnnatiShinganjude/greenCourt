Engagement Ceremoney

Last weekend, the Restaurant Green Court hosted a grand engagement event that left lasting impression on everyone who attended.
The event took place in the restaurant’s lavish banquet hall,elegantly decorated with fusion of traditional Indian and contemporary styles.
Ornate floral arrangements adorned the space, while soft lighting enhanced the romantic atmosphere, making it the perfect setting special occasion.

The evening began with a warm welcome as the couple, surrounded by their family and friends, made their entrance. Guests were greeted by hotel staff
who offered them refreshing welcome drinks and appetizers that gave a sneak peek of the culinary delights to come.
The event’s organizers had worked meticulously to ensure that every detail was perfect, from the seating arrangements to the carefully planned menu.

The engagement ceremony itself was a heartfelt affair. As the couple exchanged rings, there wasn’t a dry eye in the room.
The bride-to-be looked stunning in a traditional Indian saree, while the groom-to-be wore an elegant sherwani.
They stood before their loved ones, illuminated by the warm glow of chandeliers, while a live sitar player softly strummed in the background.
The intimate nature of the ceremony was only enhanced by the beauty of the venue and the care had been taken make the couple feel special their big day.

After the ceremony, the guests were treated to a lavish dinner buffet, featuring an array of authentic Indian dishes that highlighted the diverse flavors of the country. The chefs at the Indian Restaurant had outdone themselves, preparing everything from creamy butter paneer and aromatic biryanis to veg kebabs and freshly baked naan. The dessert table was equally impressive, offering traditional Indian sweets like gulab jamun, rasgulla, and kulfi, as well as a selection of modern desserts for those looking for something different.

Throughout the evening, the atmosphere was vibrant and festive. A live band played a mix of Indian classical and contemporary music, which kept the guests entertained as they dined and mingled.
Some even took to the dance floor, celebrating the couple's engagement with joyful dancing. The blend of live music, sumptuous food, and warm hospitality made for an unforgettable experience.

As the evening drew to a close, the couple thanked their guests for attending and expressed their gratitude to the hotel staff for making their engagement so memorable. Many of the attendees took the opportunity to compliment the restaurant’s team on the flawless execution of the event, from the impeccable service to the beautifully arranged decor. The couple and their families left with full hearts, knowing that the engagement celebration had been everything they had hoped for and more. Hotel Green Court’s Indian Restaurant had not only provided a beautiful venue but had created an experience that reflected the significance of the occasion.
The night was a perfect blend of tradition and modernity, with every detail thoughtfully planned to honor the couple’s love and cultural heritage.
It was an evening that will be cherished in the memories of all who were fortunate enough to be part of it.
