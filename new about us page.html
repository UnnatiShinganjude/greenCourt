About Us
"Welcome to The Green Court Restaurant, where plant-based cuisine meets creativity! Our chefs craft innovative dishes using the freshest vegetables, fruits, and grains. From hearty bowls and savory curries to decadent desserts and smoothies, every bite reflects compassion and sustainability. Meet Our Owner

Welcome to Green Court Restaurant, where exceptional dining meets a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Owned and operated by Mr. Raj Mhera, Green Court is renowned for its commitment to quality food and memorable dining experiences. With a deep passion for the culinary arts, Mr. Mhera has crafted a unique menu that blends traditional and modern flavors, offering something special for every guest. Under Mr. Mhera's leadership, Green Court Restaurant is dedicated to sourcing the freshest local ingredients, ensuring that every dish is a testament to quality and taste. At Green Court, we believe that great food deserves an equally great ambiance. Our restaurant offers a comfortable, elegant setting that’s perfect for family gatherings, business lunches, and casual dining. With Mr. Mhera’s vision, our goal is to provide not only delicious meals but also an unforgettable dining experience, where guests feel like part of the Green Court family. Join us at Green Court Restaurant for an unparalleled culinary journey.
